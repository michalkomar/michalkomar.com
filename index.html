What I Do

I'm an AI Consultant at J&T Banka (former Head of Digital Experience), where I lead the transformation of banking through artificial intelligence and digital innovation.

My work sits at the intersection of product strategy, AI implementation, and organizational change. I focus on deploying robust AI solutions in production, from intelligent automation to agentic systems that operate in regulated contexts. My role is to bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI capabilities and the trust and reliability that financial services demand.

Philosophy & Approach

"The future of banking isn't about replacing humans with AI - it's about augmenting human capabilities with intelligent systems that handle complexity at scale."

My approach combines three principles: Start with the customer problem, not the technology. Build systems that are transparent and explainable. And move fast, but with appropriate safeguards.

I've learned as much from failures as from successes. The wins came when we aligned incentives, moved iteratively, and focused relentlessly on measurable outcomes. We're at a rare inflection point where the technology, regulation, and market demand are all moving in the same direction.
