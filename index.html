Michal Komar is an AI consultant at J&T Banka, a Prague-based private bank, where his mandate is making generative AI safe, useful, and production-ready inside a heavily regulated environment. He is the bank's former Head of Digital Experience (2019–2025), and has spent 13+ years building digital products — as a project lead, CTO, and product manager — before moving to AI full-time in 2025.

His work sits at the intersection of product strategy, AI implementation, and organizational change: deploying robust AI solutions in production, from intelligent automation to agentic systems that operate within financial-services constraints. The job, in one sentence: bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI capabilities and the trust and reliability that regulated institutions demand.

PHILOSOPHY

"The future of banking isn't about replacing humans with AI — it's about augmenting human capabilities with intelligent systems that handle complexity at scale."
  • Start with the customer problem, not the technology. AI is a tool, not the goal.
  • Build systems that are transparent and explainable. In finance, a black box is a liability.
  • Move fast, but with appropriate safeguards. Innovation needs velocity; banking needs security.

The wins came from aligned incentives, iterative delivery, and a relentless focus on measurable outcomes — and as much was learned from the failures as from the successes.
